GoDaddy

GoDaddy is a domain hosting provider with a range of online services. It offers both email to its customers (via Microsoft 365) as well as email through their own platform (Profesional Email) email forwarding, as well as mail originating from their hosting platforms. secureserver.net is the domain used for their email servers. Any mail to or from a server with that domain will be controlled by GoDaddy. All cases provide spam filtering with very little customization, but GoDaddy's support guide on SMTP errors also includes a link to a contact form for more investigation.
